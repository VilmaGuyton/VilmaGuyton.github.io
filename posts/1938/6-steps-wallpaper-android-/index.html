Getting the perfect wallpaper for your Android Tablet can be a challenge. Fortunately, there are some simple steps that you can take to make sure that the wallpaper fits your screen perfectly. Here are 6 simple steps to get you started:

  2. Measure the tablet screen. Measure the diagonal length and the width of your tablet’s screen. This will help you to calculate the exact pixel size of the wallpaper which you should choose.

Step 2: Change wallpaper size. Go to Wallpaper and choose the picture you want to use as your wallpaper. Under the picture, select the scaling and choose Fit to screen. This will automatically adjust the wallpaper size to fit the device, thus giving you a clear wallpaper.

Step 3: Adjust picture size. Once the wallpaper is selected, you can further adjust the picture size using the Center, Fit To Screen, Stretch, Tile or Crop options. Select the one that best suits your tablet. Note that choosing the Stretch option will cause the wallpaper to be filled with unwanted black bars.

Finally, press the Done button. The new wallpaper should now be set and the size should fit the tablet screen correctly. With these steps, you now know how to make your wallpaper fit the screen of your Android tablet.
