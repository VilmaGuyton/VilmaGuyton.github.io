Finally, if you own a device with a microSD card, consider transferring some of the data to the card. This will help you free up storage space on your device. Remember, it is important to back up your data before clearing caches, deleting apps, or performing factory reset.

By following these steps, you can clear your Gmail app cache on Android and increase the speed and memory of your device. Additionally, clearing your cache periodically can help maintain the smooth running of your device.
