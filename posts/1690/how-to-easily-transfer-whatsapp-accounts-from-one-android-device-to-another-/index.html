Here’s how to do it.

First, open up your old Android device and go to “Settings”. Then, scroll down and select “Chat” and click on the “Back Up Chat” option. This will create a backup of all the chats and data stored on your old device. Next, log in to your Google Drive or any cloud storage you prefer, and copy the backup from your device.

Now, switch to your new device and sign in to the same Google Drive or cloud storage that you used on the old device. Download the backup file and save it in the WhatsApp folder. After that, open up the WhatsApp app on your new device and tap on the “Restore” option. This will restore all of your chats and data from the backup file.

And that’s it! With these simple steps, you can easily and quickly transfer your WhatsApp account from one Android device to another. So no need to worry about losing your important data anymore!
