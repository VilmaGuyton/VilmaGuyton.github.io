1. Prepare the iPhone#

Writing a blog post has never been easier, especially in the age of the iPhone 6s. Taking a screenshot is an important function of any mobile device, and it’s especially useful on the iPhone 6s. To take a screenshot without the home button, follow these steps. First, unlock your phone. With these easy steps, you can take a screenshot on your iPhone 6s in no time.

-Learn How to Take a Screenshot On iPhone 6s#

Taking a screenshot on an iPhone 6s can be done easily, even without the Home button. All you need is to prepare your iPhone first. Firstly, make sure the mute switch is off, the ring/silent switch can be found on the upper left hand side of your iPhone 6s. Then, go to the screen that you wish to take a screenshot of. Finally, press both the Sleep/Wake button on the right side and the volume up button on the left side at the same time. You should hear a shutter sound and see a white flash on the screen to indicate that the screenshot has been taken.

-Tips and Tricks to Capture Screenshots Without Home Button#

Taking a screenshot on iPhone 6s without home button can be tricky. As this model does not come with a home button, there are some different steps to take a screenshot. First, make sure that the screen you want to capture is in view. Then, press and hold the ‘Power’ button located on the side of the device. While still pressing the power button, press and release the ‘Volume Up’ button on the opposite side of the device. You should see the screen animate and a preview of the captured screenshot will appear in the bottom left corner of the screen. Finally, go to the preview to edit or save the screenshot. With these steps, you will be able to take a screenshot on your iPhone 6s without home button.

2. Activate Assistive Touch Feature#

Are you an iPhone 6s user? Did you know there is a way to take screenshot without using the Home button? You can do it by activating iPhone 6s assistive touch feature. This feature allows you to use on-screen buttons, like screenshot feature, that can be used as a substitute for the physical home button.

To activate assistive touch, open Settings, then go to General, and choose Accessibility. from there, you can find Assistive Touch. tap on it, and turn on the switch. You will notice an icon of a white floating button on the side of your iPhone’s screen.

To take a screenshot, simply tap the assistive touch button, and find screenshot. tap it , and voila! You will hear a sound of iPhone taking a picture, as it will be saved to your Camera Roll.

You can also use this feature to do other tasks, such as turning off your phone, volume control, taking a lock screen screenshot, and setting text size. There is no need to worry if you accidentally close the assistive touch button. you can easily turn it back on by going to Settings and find it in Accessibility menu.

1. Introduction to Assistive Touch#

Taking a screenshot on an iPhone 6s without a home button can be a struggle. Fortunately, Apple has added the Assistive Touch feature which makes it much easier. With Assistive Touch, you can take screenshots quickly and easily. To activate this feature, navigate to Settings > General > Accessibility > Assistive Touch > toggle Assistive Touch ON. Once enabled, a small circle will appear onscreen that can be repositioned to any area. To take a screenshot, simply press and hold on the Assistive Touch circle and choose Screenshot from the menu. Your iPhone 6s will immediately capture a screenshot and save it in the Photos app. With Assistive Touch, taking a screenshot on an iPhone 6s without a home button is just as easy as with any other iPhone.

2. Activating Assistive Touch Feature on iPhone 6s#

The iPhone 6s has no home button anymore, so how do you take a screenshot on the device? Thankfully, Apple has included an Assistive Touch feature on all of its newer devices to help with this task. Here’s how to activate it and take a screenshot:

Open the Settings app on your device. Scroll down to Accessibility and tap it open. Tap Touch in the Accessibility menu. Scroll down to the Interaction section and tap Assistive Touch. Toggle Assistive Touch to the ON position.

A white Assistive Touch button should appear on the screen. Tap this button and a menu will appear. Tap Device in this menu and tap More. Tap Screenshot to take a screenshot of the current display.

You can also use the Assistive Touch button to access the Home, Lock Screen, Notifications, Control Center, and Siri features. This makes your iPhone 6s easier to use without the home button, which can be a challenge for some people.
