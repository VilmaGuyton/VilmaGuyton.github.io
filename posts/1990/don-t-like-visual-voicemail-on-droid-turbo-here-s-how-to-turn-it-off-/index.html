1. Explanation of Visual Voicemail on Droid Turbo#

Visual voicemail allows you to access your voicemails without having to listen to them in the order they were received. This feature is available on the Droid Turbo, but if it isn’t your thing then you can easily turn it off. Here’s how:

First, open up your Droid Turbo phone app. Then, tap on the three dots in the upper right corner and select ‘Settings’. From there, select ‘Voicemail’. On this screen, you’ll see the option to turn off Visual Voicemail, so just slide the toggle switch to the ‘off’ position.

After turning off Visual Voicemail, you will still see messages that you have not yet listened to, but you will have to listen to them in the order they were received. You also won’t be able to use the ‘fast forward’ or ‘rewind’ features at all.

Lastly, once you have successfully turned off Visual Voicemail on your Droid Turbo, make sure that you don’t forget your voicemail password. Your voicemail password is used to access certain features, such as deleting voicemails or setting up notifications.
