1. Overview of Visual Voicemail#

Visual Voicemail gives you a simplified way to manage your voicemails. You can see the list of messages, pick the one you want to listen to and delete, all without having to dial a number. However, if you’re having trouble with visual voicemail, or don’t want the feature anymore, you can easily turn it off from your Droid Turbo device. Here are the five steps to turn off visual voicemail on your Droid Turbo:

  1. Go to the main settings page of your Droid Turbo, then tap “Device” > “Apps”.

  2. Scroll down the list and select “Voicemail”.

  3. Now select “More” and tap “Visual Voicemail Settings”.

  4. Finally, uncheck “Enable Visual Voicemail” and hit “Ok”.

That’s it! Your visual voicemail will now be turned off and you’ll be able to access your voicemails through the traditional method. If you ever decide to turn your visual voicemail back on, just follow these five steps again and check the box for “Enable Visual Voicemail”.
