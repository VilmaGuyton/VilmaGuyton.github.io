1. Overview of Steps to Check AirPods Battery#

If you have AirPods and an Android device, this guide will show you how to check the battery level of your AirPods. To check your AirPods battery, follow these simple steps:

  1. Swipe down on your Android device to open the Quick Settings panel.
  2. Tap the Bluetooth icon.
  3. Tap the information (i) icon next to AirPods.
  4. The battery level of both AirPods will be displayed here.

That’s it! You can now check the battery level of your AirPods anytime to ensure you get the most out of them. Keep in mind that you can also check the battery level of your AirPods on your iPhone or iPad instead of using an Android device.
