Bit rate is also an important factor when recording video on your Android phone. It is the amount of data used to encode one second of the video. The last factor that affects the video recording length is the type of codec used. Some of the most popular codecs are h.264 and h.265; those are compatible with a wide range of Android phones and provides good video quality with less space requirements.
