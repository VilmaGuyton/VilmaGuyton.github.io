1.Tips for Improving Camera Settings#

For Android users who are looking to capture longer videos, mastering your camera settings is key. To capture smooth videos for a longer period of time, try these helpful tips:

First, adjust your resolution. When filming for a longer time, it can be important to lower the resolution to stretch the recording time because higher resolution videos take up more storage space.

Second, turn off the auto focus. This feature will continuously adjust and refocus your lens while filming a video. When filming for a longer video, it’s important to turn off the auto focus to prevent glitches and the focus from changing.

Third, use a higher frame rate. Setting your frame rate to higher levels can help you capture smoother videos. Higher frame rates provide a better quality of video and can help your video capture smoothly for a longer period of time.

Finally, reduce the video bitrate. The higher the bitrate, the more data will be used and the larger the video size will be. To record longer videos, reduce the bitrate in order to save storage space and extend the video duration.
