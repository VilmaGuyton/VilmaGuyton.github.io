Q2 : Can I take a screenshot if I am using MacOS?

A2 : Yes, you can take a screenshot if you are using MacOS. All you have to do is press and hold Command + Shift + 3 on your keyboard. This will take a screenshot of your entire screen, including the video call window. The image will be saved to your computer’s Desktop folder.

Q3 : Can I take a screenshot of a specific area of my screen?

A3 : Yes, you can take a screenshot of a specific area of your screen. All you have to do is press and hold Command + Shift + 4 on your keyboard. You can then select the area that you want to screenshot. The image will be saved to your computer’s Desktop folder.
